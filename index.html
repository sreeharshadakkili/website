About Me

I'm a Senior Data Systems Analyst at Tech Goes Home, a nonprofit dedicated to closing the digital divide. With a Master's in Business Analytics from UMass Boston, I combine technical expertise with a passion for social impact.

Throughout my career, I've contributed to various causes including the elimination of child sexual abuse, food insecurity, urban poverty, digital divide, and AI literacy. I have a knack for solving complex problems by leveraging both my technical and business skills.

When I'm not working with data, I love spending time in nature and seeking out adventure activities. I'm also a credit card enthusiast who enjoys optimizing rewards and benefits programs.
